This forum has been established to meet the diverse technology needs of the Orthodox community. Whether you're seeking information on kosher phones or exploring coding and other tech topics, we strive to provide a comprehensive resource for all your digital needs.

Our forum is full of information ranging from information and guides to beginner questions. We have a wonderful community that is eager to help others and share their contributions. You can find shared apps, guides, and more - all put out there by your fellow users just to help you!

The apps page is a list of compiled apps from our community forum's Uploaded Apps Thread. Users share (modded) apps that are Kosher and optimized for you to be able to easily use.
